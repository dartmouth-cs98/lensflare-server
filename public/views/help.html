Annotate your world


Lensflare is a Hololens applicaiton built to provide curation solutions for every need, from car dealerships to art museums to your own home. Set up is designed to be simple and straight forward: follow the directions below to get started.
Start by creating a Lensflare account, if you don't already have one. Once it's made, add a new space by clicking "add new space" in the sidebar and then create your first device by clicking "manage my devices" and then "new device" - select the space you just made to associate your HoloLens with that space.
